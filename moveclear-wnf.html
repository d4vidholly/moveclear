WNF Manager

Fair teams. Clear picks. No arguments.

Wednesday night football is chaotic. Too many players, too many opinions, not enough time. WNF Manager takes a pool of players — with positions and ratings — and builds balanced, fair teams automatically. The organiser gets clarity. The group gets a game they trust. Everyone plays.

The challenge

What wasn't working.

01

The organiser burden

Someone always ends up managing the WhatsApp group, tracking who's in, manually picking teams in their head. It's thankless, it's slow, and it's always the same person.

02

Teams never feel fair

Random picks, blind randomisers, and the captain-pick system all produce lopsided games. A 6-1 feels bad for everyone. The group loses trust in the process before the match even starts.

03

No position awareness

Existing team-picker apps treat all players as equal. Putting three goalkeepers on one side and none on the other is technically "balanced." It needed to understand positions.

04

Different numbers each week

Ten this week. Fourteen next. A late drop-out at 6pm. Any tool that couldn't handle fluid player pools in real time wasn't going to survive contact with an actual Wednesday night.

The approach

How we built it.

1

Rating + position model

Each player in the pool has a skill rating and a preferred position. The algorithm balances teams across both dimensions simultaneously — not just total ability, but positional distribution too.

2

Fluid player pool

Players can be added or removed from the pool right up to kick-off. The tool regenerates balanced teams from whatever the final confirmed list is — late drop-outs don't break the system.

3

Formation view

Generated teams are displayed in a tactical formation layout — not just a list of names. The organiser can see whether the teams make sense as a shape, not just as a set of numbers.

4

No accounts needed

Like all Move Clear products, WNF Manager is built for the moment. Open it on your phone in the car park, build your teams, show the group. Done. No signup, no setup, no friction.

What's in it

Key features.

Rating-based team balance

Algorithm distributes players across teams to equalise total skill ratings — minimising the gap between the sides before a ball is kicked.

Position-aware distribution

Positions are factored into team building — ensuring each side has a natural shape rather than three strikers and no cover at the back.

Formation view

See each team laid out in their tactical formation, not just a list. Spot imbalances at a glance and regenerate if needed before stepping out.

Saved player pool

Your regular players are remembered — add this week's confirmed names, remove drop-outs, and generate teams from your actual lineup in seconds.

What's next

On the roadmap.

Result tracking

Record scores after each game — building a history of results that feeds back into player ratings over time, based on actual game outcomes.

Player performance data

Goal and assist tracking per player — a running record that creates real data-driven ratings rather than manually assigned ones.

Group sharing

Generate a shareable link to the week's teams — so the organiser can send the lineup to the group WhatsApp without screenshots or retyping.

Season-end report

An end-of-season summary for the regular game: top scorer, most appearances, form table — data that makes the grassroots game feel like it counts.
